The gods are dead.

At least, that’s what everyone else seems to think.

I, however, know the truth. My father, the God of Ruin, is coming, and with him, everything will fall.

Hiding out at this dangerous academy can only protect me for so long, even if I am disguised as a boy to avoid the brutal Bride Trials.

One of the cruel princes of the school, Killian, watches me from the shadows.

Waiting.

Biding his time.

I fear that when he catches me, he’ll either be my salvation…

Or my downfall.

RH??

It’s a great book with stellar writing. I love Little Bob, the FMC. Ends on a cliffhanger but not unbearable. Unique plot and I enjoy a good ‘pretend to be a man’ storyline.
Initially when I began the book, I thought I wouldn’t enjoy the Little Bob/Sy split. The FMC has a sentient succubus type being that she can exchange places with called Sy. I thought it would be like having two FMC’s in one body but fortunately we are narrated through Little Bob and Sy isn’t a distraction. I ended up loving both Little Bob and Sy.
My overall concern with the series is the harem. It doesn’t seem to be a true RH. There are 5 potential candidates, all of them Princes in the last remaining magical realm. However Little Bob only seems to be attracted to one of the them, Prince Killian of the Chaos house. I don’t like when harems have an obvious favorite so I’m hoping that there’s a valid explanation for this (I have some guesses) and the rest of the harem get equal respect. Prince Killian is also stronger than the rest of the Princes’ and I don’t care for the power imbalance. I like the Vampire Prince, Louis, but I don’t like books where it seems like the male harem members would seriously hurt the FMC. Slight spoilers ahead. He did almost drain her but I guess that’s forgivable. I did not like it at all when the shifter prince Silas backhanded her and it’d take some groveling for me to be okay with him again. I like the Fae Prince Rowan, he’s my favorite but that’s probably because he hasn’t had the chance to be mean to Little Bob yet since he’s barely in the book. It also helps that Sy seems to really like him. Killian used to be my favorite but he lost major points with that stunt he pulled at the end.
So far the evidence points to the harem only being three members despite Little Bob & Sy having expressed even the slightest bit of interest in all five. Most likely the Fae Prince Rowan, the Vampire Prince Louis, and the Chaos Prince Killian. Partially because that’s what the three book titles are based around and that’s who she seems the most interested in. Hoping for more Romance in the next because there is absolutely none here. I prefer books where it seems like there will be a HEA (happily ever after) or at least seems like the harem will care and love the FMC.
