3.5 stars

This is the third book in the Amy Bensen series (definitely not a standalone) and veers away from Amy’s story to feature her brother Chad. Up until the end of the last book, Amy (and the reader) thought that Chad died 6 years ago in the fire that killed her parents, but it turns out he is very much alive and has been the one orchestrating Amy’s protection from afar and working to keep her hidden from the men who killed their family and who are still on the hunt for the two of them.

Chad is a treasure-hunter and is the one who holds the secret behind all of the mystery. He knows what the bad guys want, he knows why his family was killed and he knows why he and Amy continue to be targets. So finally, we’re getting some answers!

The book is told entirely from Chad’s POV and begins with him being rescued from captivity and torture by the mysterious Gia. She promises to help him, asking nothing in return except for his protection, but who is she really?

I really liked Chad. He’s just a regular guy who has been thrown into hideous circumstances and has had to adapt to survive. He’s done things he never thought himself capable of and has become cynical and jaded, and distrustful of pretty much everybody he encounters. Gia is no exception.

The two of them go on the road together as Chad works to track down his sister, and despite all of the questions and doubt, there is an underlying tension between them and they are unable to resist their sizzling chemistry.

Wrapping my hand around her neck, I pull her to me, flattening her body against mine, bringing her mouth a breath away from the next kiss I've denied myself too long. "I don't care who's going to hate who later. I just want to fuck you."
She curls her fingers around my shirt. "Then stop talking and do it."
"You can't handle this part of me."
Her chin lifts defiantly. "Try me."

Chad and Gia together are on fire! Chad has some dominant tendencies, but nothing too extreme, and the sex soon leads to emotions that Chad is in no way prepared to deal with. He is a man with only two goals – protect his sister and get his revenge on the men who destroyed his world. He is constantly trying to figure out if those close to him are betraying him, and things become super complicated when he and Gia start to fall for each other, especially when he knows that she is keeping secrets from him.

I really enjoyed this read. It answers a lot of questions, and brings the action and mystery of the whole series to a head when Chad is finally reunited with Amy (and now Liam) and they work together to finish their nightmare once and for all. The character dynamics are great as all of the strong personalities clash (the Chad/Liam interactions are hilarious) and the whole thing moves at a great pace with lots of twists and turns and some great surprises.

With lots of action, drama, mystery excitement and a sexy romance, this book progresses the series nicely and is a really enjoyable read.

3.5 stars.

Note - I listened to this as an audiobook narrated by the brilliant Sebastian York and, as always, he did a great job with it. I highly recommend listening to this one.

Forsaken has been one of my most anticipated reads. I have been waiting patiently for two years for more answers and now everything finally comes together.

Forsaken is Chad’s story. Chad is Amy’s brother. The men who originally hired Chad to find the treasure, have taken him and want the treasure for themselves. When Gia finds out what plans the men have for Chad, she sets her own plan in motion to help him escape. Now the two of them are on the run. Motives will be questioned. Trust will be fleeting. This game of cat and mouse will leave you asking even more question than before…

I am not going to go into a lot of details, but I will tell you this: We finally, finally get some answers. We finally find out what happened and how Amy got sent into hiding. Chad had to risk so much to not only keep Amy safe and alive, but to also keep himself safe. And let me tell you, this story takes you on one thrilling ride.

I did have a few issues with the story. In the beginning, I did find it a little hard to follow. The writing transitions weren’t very smooth, making it hard to tell who was talking at times. My other issue was the ending. I felt like it was a bit rushed. The story hit it’s climax and then it was like boom, it’s over. Please don’t me wrong. I know there is a book 4 coming out soon and is supposed to be wrapping up the series as a whole. I just felt like something was missing. I guess I was expecting a little more closure with Chad’s part of the story since this is his book.

All that being said, I did really enjoy this book. It was fast paced and action packed. I was pulled into the story immediately and couldn’t wait to see what would happen next. There definitely was never a dull moment. I loved the twists. The twists kept me guessing and on the edge of my seat. There was also plenty of romance and steamy moments. Amy and Liam fans, do not fret. We do get spend plenty of time with them.

Everything has come full circle now. I still have some questions and am waiting in anticipation to get those answers. I can’t wait to see how the rest of the story will unfold and find out what’s in store for everyone next.
