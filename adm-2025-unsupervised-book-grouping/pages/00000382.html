Jason Drake #1

The Assassin's Betrayal

Auston King

4.29
5,924 ratings168 reviews
Rate this book
Jason Drake isn't just ex-CIA.
He's a wanted man.
And now he's the world's last hope to stop a world war.

A weapon equivalent to a small nuclear bomb is missing.
Not one intelligence agency in the world has a lead.
Everyone who gets close to the truth ends up dead.

With no one to trust at the Agency and knowing that time is of the essence, CIA Station Chief Kate Price turns to the only man she knows she can trust: disgraced covert operative and former assassin Jason Drake.

The problem? Drake is a wanted man.

What follows is a globe-trotting thriller ripped straight from the headlines. It's a story of intrigue, non-stop action, friends turned enemies, and betrayal.

The Assassin's Betrayal kicks off one of the most full-throttle CIA assassin series to hit bookstores in decades. It was created from the ground up to fill the space between Jason Bourne and Mitch Rapp.

This series of espionage thrillers is ready to shake you to the core and keep you reading late into the night. Auston's primary goal is to share stories that entertain, provoke, and leave you wanting more.

Are you ready to read the best thriller novel of 2023?
